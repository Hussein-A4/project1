Why I enjoy Volleyball
Volleyball is played on a court divided into two sides by a net, with six players on each of the two teams. The objective in volleyball is to send the ball over the net so that the other side canâ€™t return it. Each team has three attempts to hit the ball over the net. When a serve is hit by the server over the net to the opposing team the ball is put into play. If the receiving team wins the rally, they get the chance to serve, and the players on their team rotate clockwise once. If the serving team wins the rally, they win a point and serve again. The ball must clear the net on the serve, and the players play up to 25 or 15 points per set.
Like any other sports, there are rules that need to be followed.
  1. The game must start with a serve from the side that has won a point(if starting from 0-0 doesnt matter which side starts)
  2. The team receiving the ball is allowed 3 hits, those being either a bump, a set, or a spike. Using the 3 hits, the goal is to get the ball over the net. If more then 3 hits are used the play is over and whoever wins the point serves again.
  3. When breaking the enemy teams streak, your team will serve and rotate clockwise
Volleyball is a very competitive and difficult sport, and I enjoy sports that have a challenge to them. Having a challenge always motivates me to practice and improve. Playing volleyball can help improve a lot of skills, including movement, learn to serve, pass, and spike the ball. Volleyball can also improve communication skills when having to work with teamates. The sport is very unique compared to other sports. There are many variables that need to be taken into consideration making the sport hard and challenging. Overall the sport can be very fun espcially when you learn more about it.
